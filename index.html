About Me

I am an Research Assistant at the AGenCy Lab under the AI & ML wing of Center for Computational & Data Sciences, Independent University, Bangladesh (IUB) under the supervision of Prof. M. Ashraful Amin & Dr. Amin Ahsan Ali. I am also working as a Senior Engineer, MLOps at Quantigo AI. Before starting my industry career, I received my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from Independent University, Bangladesh, and served as a Graduate Research Assistant at the AGenCy Lab for three years.

The field of my research encompasses a broad domain of Human-Computer Interaction, Extended Reality, and Interactive Machine Learning. I aim to build AI-driven intelligent interactive robust 3D user interface and extended reality universe, to deal with crucial problems for both individual and community.
